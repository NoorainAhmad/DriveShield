About DriveShield

The DriveShield Dashboard is a comprehensive platform tailored for managing vehicle insurance records, specifically designed for underwriters and administrators. It facilitates the seamless creation, viewing, and administration of insurance policies, user registrations, and access control.

The system delineates two key roles: Admin and Underwriter, each endowed with distinct privileges and functionalities. Admins can manage underwriter registrations and deletions, while underwriters are empowered to manage vehicle insurance policies, search records, and update passwords.
